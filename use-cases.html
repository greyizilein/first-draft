Prompt comparison

The difference between generic prompting and engineered prompting is structural, not stylistic. In this case, the outcome is in the details:

Generic vs engineered prompts

Write a literature review on the impact of social media on students. Make it detailed and well-structured. Use academic sources.
You are an academic writer. TASK: Generate a literature review section only. CONSTRAINTS: – Define key theoretical frameworks – Compare conflicting findings – Identify methodological gaps – Maintain critical evaluation throughout STRUCTURE: Proceed section by section. Do not summarise sources sequentially. Stop after synthesis.

Why engineered prompts outperform outsourcing

Paying for work removes control, introduces risk, and does not scale. Engineered prompts preserve ownership and are much cheaper while eliminating inefficiencies.

Lower long-term cost

One engineered prompt system replaces repeated payments for outsourced work.

Higher academic integrity

You create the work. The system enforces structure and standards.

Reusable infrastructure

Prompts can be reused, refined, and adapted across projects of a similar class.
