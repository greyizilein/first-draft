Our Method

First Draft does not generate your entire work. Our goal is to significantly reduce the amount of payment in outsourcing your work by designing system prompts specifically focused on executing your task immediately our prompt is run on the specified AI systems or models. We generate structured instruction systems that control how your AI produces output, so you can work faster without compromising authorship, time, or cost.

What a prompt is

Prompts are instructions, not solutions

Prompts are instructions that tell an AI how to think, standards to apply, what structure to follow, and where to stop. Poor prompts produce vague, generic output. Engineered prompts like ours produce better, repeatable results.

Most people use prompts incorrectly

Typical prompts are short, ambiguous, and underspecified. They rely on the AI to guess expectations, leading to surface-level outputs and extensive reworking.

What engineered prompts change

Engineered prompts encode domain or genre-specific logic directly into their instructions. They define role, scope, depth, structure, citation rules, and stopping conditions before the work begins.

First Draft prompts systems

They are context-aware

Each prompt is built for a specific domain/genre based on specific requirements provided to us from your request, with expectations aligned to those specifics alone.

They control depth and boundaries

Our prompt systems instruct the AI on what to include, what to exclude, how far to go, and when to stop, preventing overreach, repetition, and fluff/fillers.

They preserve authorship

Your work is the solution. Our prompts are the guidance, structure, and analytical framing that you require to create the perfect solution. You remain the author, while the system supports thinking.

Why this is easier, cheaper, and BETTER

Paying for a full outsourced work also outsources thinking, risks integrity breaches, reduces your input/voice and scales poorly. Engineered prompts reduce effort while increasing control.

The practical advantage

Faster execution

Clear instructions eliminate trial-and-error prompting and repeated reworking.

Lower cost

One engineered prompt system replaces ongoing payments for the full outsourced work.

Higher integrity

You produce the work. The system simply removes ambiguity and inefficiency.

Why First Draft?

If you want control, speed, clarity, and personal credibility, without outsourcing authorship of your work or the process, our engineered prompts are the right tool.
