What First Draft does

Most tools generate text. First Draft generates instructions. You stay in control of the writing process, while the AI follows a clear academic plan.

Executable

Outputs are ready-to-paste prompts that specify structure, depth, constraints, and stop points.

Academic-grade

Prompts scale by level (assignment → PhD) and adapt to discipline and methodology.

Customisable

Choose citation style, scope (single section or full work), and the chapter/section you need.

Academic integrity: This tool outputs prompts (instructions), not completed submissions. You remain responsible for originality, accuracy, and referencing.

How it works

Three steps: define → generate → use anywhere.

1

Describe your task

Select level, work type, scope, discipline, and your topic (plus word count and citation style if needed).

2

Get a structured prompt

Receive an advanced prompt that tells an AI writer what to do, how to structure it, and when to stop.

3

Use it anywhere

Paste the prompt into your preferred AI tool. Review, revise, and guide the output section-by-section.

Important

First Draft generates prompts only. It does not generate your academic work.
